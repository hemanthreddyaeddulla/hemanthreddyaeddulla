### Selected projects

**Quant research / signals**

- <a href="https://github.com/hemanthreddyaeddulla/lob-price-prediction" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><code>lob-price-prediction</code></a> - Real-time limit order book price-movement prediction. **53 of 4,900+ (top 1.1%)** in the Wunder Predictorium competition. 10-model GRU ensemble, 158 O(1) engineered features, stateful ONNX inference at ~1.6 ms/step on one vCPU.
- <a href="https://github.com/hemanthreddyaeddulla/vol-decomposition-har" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><code>vol-decomposition-har</code></a> - HAR realized-variance forecasting that decomposes daily RV into overnight and intraday components. Walk-forward out-of-sample on SPY, QQQ, IWM, AAPL, JPM from 2010 to 2026, with a shuffled-overnight placebo to rule out spurious signal.
- <a href="https://github.com/hemanthreddyaeddulla/march-madness-2026" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><code>march-madness-2026</code></a> - Kaggle March ML Mania 2026. **65 of 3,462 teams (12,252 entrants) - top 2%.** Brier 0.1229 vs 0.1097 for 1st place. 6-model stacking ensemble with OOF-optimized weights and gender-specific isotonic calibration.

**Market risk**

- <a href="https://github.com/hemanthreddyaeddulla/FRTB-SA-Capital-Engine" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><code>FRTB-SA-Capital-Engine</code></a> - Basel III FRTB Standardised Approach capital engine covering GIRR, FX, and Equity Delta risk classes. Sensitivity-based method aggregation across BASE, HIGH, and LOW correlation scenarios, with full traceability to MAR21 paragraphs.
- <a href="https://github.com/hemanthreddyaeddulla/market-risk-var-backtesting" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><code>market-risk-var-backtesting</code></a> - VaR framework with five methodologies (Parametric, Historical, Monte Carlo, Factor-based, GARCH), Expected Shortfall at 97.5% and 99%, Basel III traffic-light backtesting, Kupiec POF, Christoffersen independence, stress testing, and Component VaR decomposition.

**Credit risk**

- <a href="https://github.com/hemanthreddyaeddulla/alternative-data-credit-scoring" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><code>alternative-data-credit-scoring</code></a> - Credit-risk pipeline benchmarking traditional bureau data against alternative sources (digital footprint, behavioral signals, external scores). LightGBM at 0.77 AUC and 84% acceptance rate at a 5% bad-rate cutoff. Includes thin-file customer analysis.
